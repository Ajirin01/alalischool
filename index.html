We Are Professional Builders

At Al-ali international school, we believe in the transformative power of education. Located in the heart of
F-Layout in Minna, our institution stands as a beacon of academic excellence, character development, and
holistic growth for students aged 2 to 18 years of age. Established with a vision to nurture young minds and
equip them with the skills necessary to thrive in an ever-evolving world, AIS offers a dynamic and
comprehensive educational experience. Our dedicated managers are committed to providing a supportive
learning environment where pupils are encouraged to explore their interests, challenge themselves, and
reach their full potential.

Our rigorous academic curriculum is designed to stimulate intellectual curiosity and foster critical thinking
skills. From core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Humanities to a wide array of subjects and enrichment programs, we offer a diverse range of learning opportunities tailored to meet the needs and interests of every student. Our state-of-the-art learning environment provides students with access to
cutting-edge facilities and resources, including modern classrooms, science labs, computer labs, a library,
sports facilities, and more. We are committed to creating a conducive learning environment that inspires
creativity, innovation, and academic excellence.

We believe in instilling strong values and principles in our students, shaping them into responsible and
compassionate individuals. Through our comprehensive character education program, we emphasize the
importance of integrity, empathy, and respect for others, preparing students to become ethical leaders and
active contributors to society.

We have been proudly serving Niger state and its environs for 11 years. Since 2013, we have built lives that are high fliers in areas such as:

ICT

We purposefully integrate ICT into our pupils with optimal fusion of intelligence, process and technology to enhance solution development skill.

Medicine

We establish and develop the foundation for the pupils to explore and break grounds in the field of medicine anywhere in the world.

Science

With our state-of-the-art science lab, we provide adequate learning facilities that triggers scientific inquisitiveness, abilities and prowess.

Politics

We educate and enlightens our pupils to be good leaders and politicians that will foster peace and good governance across the Nigerian divide .
