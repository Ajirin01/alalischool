ADMISSION PROCEDURES

AIS admits children between the ages of (18 months to 11 years) for maximum comfort and joyful learning, a beautiful learning environment/ ( class rooms) are provided to accommodate 20 children (max)

PLEASE NOTE THAT FOR ADMISSIONS INTO THE NURSERY, WE HAVE A TEAM INTAKE BEGINNING THE TERM FOLLOWING YOUR CHILD'S THIRD BIRTHDAY.

Enrollment Procedure

After completion of the school tour, the application form would be bought, filled and returned to the school. The parents of the child will have an interview which enables the school listen to and understand the expectations of the parents from both the school and the child. If this interview ends successfully, the parents are then required to pay a registration fee. The registration package includes the following, Enrollment Agreement Medical Form Parent commitment form preschool intake form ( where applicable) Child's ID card or pickup permit consent forms ALL THESE FORMS MUST BE FILLED AND RETURNED WITH EVIDENCE OF COMPLETE [AYMENT OF PRESENT TERM SCHOOL FEES BEFORE SPACE IS GIVEN TO THE CHILD.

Payment Procedures

Fees to be paid will be communicated to the parents. Al-Ali school maintains a no cash policy. We accept bank drafts and cheques. Bank tellers are available at the front desk. Fees are not due later than drop off time the first day of school. Fees may also be paid in advance. Any child whose fees are not paid on time will not be allowed in the school.
